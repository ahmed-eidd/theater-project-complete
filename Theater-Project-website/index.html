About UPSTAGE

WHAT WE DO

Upstage is the direct result of being a theatre company owner.

As a theatre professional who is passionate about the stage, I countinuously faced problem after problem trying to get a show off the ground

Some of these included finding the right theatre space to produce the show finding the right actors,finding a production team, finding sponsors and everything in between.

As a direct result of these challenges, I have created a prodcut that will be the one stop shop for theatre professionals

A platform that will handle all aspects of theatre prodcution needs from the writing of the play to the close of the curtain.

Upstage will be the platform that all theatre professionals will use to manage their careers, performances, actiors, production teams and help market performances to the public.
